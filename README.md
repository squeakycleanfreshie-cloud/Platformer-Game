# Platformer-Game
A simple one screen, scrolling platformer. All levels designed by me, assets made by brakeyes asset pack. You play as a knight who is exploring a new world. Adventure through lots of different biomes, platfromes and mazes.

## Why I made this?
I made this during the horizons cruz godot workshop, and I learned lots of fun stuff

## How to play
Click this link: https://remymorea.itch.io/knight-platformer
Controls: WASD or Arrow Keys

## Features
Features one level or continuous levels, and diferent appearance biomes.
